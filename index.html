Profit Sharing Agreement:

  • All profits earned from the investment will be shared on a 50/50 basis between the investor and PGA after the total investment capital has been returned to the investor.
  • Profit sharing applies only to the amount earned beyond the initial capital invested.
2.

Capital Safety Guarantee:

  • The trader (PGA) is responsible for managing investments, but all trading strategies and decisions will depend solely on the trader’s expertise.
  • Investors are assured of capital safety, as the trader’s priority will be returning the total invested amount before profit sharing begins.
3.

Loss Disclaimer:

  • Investors must acknowledge that all investment activities carry market risks, including potential losses, and returns are subject to market conditions.
  • However, the trader commits to minimizing risks using diversified trading strategies.
  • Any losses will be recovered first before profit sharing begins.
4.

Investor Responsibilities:

  • The investor agrees to the terms of the minimum contract period and weekly payout structure.
  • Early withdrawals or cancellations will forfeit ongoing profit sharing unless expressly agreed upon.
5.

Capital Recovery Clause:

  • In the unlikely event of market losses, the trader guarantees that all efforts will be made to recover the initial capital.
  • Profit sharing will only commence once the initial capital has been fully restored.
